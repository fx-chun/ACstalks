ACstalks Guidelines

Please remember your password

When you first sign up, we do not have a way for you to retrieve your password by yourself if you have forgotten it. If you need help retrieving your password please email us at dev@acstalks.com and let us know that you need a password reset. Alternatively, you can create a new account and email us to delete the abandoned account. You will need to provide the username for the account.

Please use your Bio accordingly

Your Bio is a place for you to write a few things about you and your own guidelines that you may have. Some examples of using your Bio are:

  • A link to a social media account (e.g. Twitter, Instagram, Etsy)
  • Your procedure for inviting people to your island
  • A way to contact you (e.g. Discord ID, Twitter message,)
  • Something about tips (e.g. "I appreciate any tips" or "Don't tip me")
  • You can self promote (e.g. artwork, fanfics, please do not link to anything NSFW)

Do not spam/create false prices

Please do not create false pricing/false Dodo Codes for your island/false Nintendo Switch Friend Codes. If you see someone doing this please report them to dev@acstalks.com

Be respectful and courteous

If someone you don't know allows you to come to their island for whatever reason, please be respectful of their island. Do not run through or dig up their flowers, do not cut down their trees, do not leave your trash around on their island, and do not harvest their rocks. The person inviting you is doing them a favor, please treat them respectfully. In addition, some people may bring an item or tip bells as a "thank you" for the transaction, and this is a kind action but not required nor should be expected. Please do not harass others for tips, tipping is not a required action.
Also, if you're the one opening your island please do not troll people with crazy fences or other obstacles that will impede them from their necessary function(s) on your island.

Not liable for the people you might meet

We are in no way responsible for the potential people you may meet/their Animal Crossing islands. Please remember that when you use Nintendo's online service for the Switch you sometiems may be subjected to inappropriate creations made by other people. Please refer to the Nintendo Switch Online policies. We ask that everyone be respectful and courteous to others.

Health and Safety

Please refer to Nintendo's Health and Safety Information on how to stay physically healthy and safe during the times you are playing on the Switch.
